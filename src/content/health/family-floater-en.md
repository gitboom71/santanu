# Family Floater Health Insurance

Unlike individual plans where each person has a dedicated limit, a **Family Floater** shares a single pool of coverage across all covered family members.

### Key Features
* **Shared Sum Insured:** Covers spouse and children under one limit.
* **Cost Effective:** Lower total premium than buying separate policies.
* **Restoration Benefit:** Automatically refills your sum insured if exhausted.
