Buying insurance without reading the fine print can lead to surprises when you actually need to claim.

### 1. Underinsuring for Medical Inflation
Medical care costs rise fast. A 5 Lakh policy that felt sufficient five years ago might leave a gap during a major hospital stay today.

### 2. Concealing Pre-existing Conditions
Always disclose past medical conditions truthfully—hiding them is the primary reason claims get rejected.

### 3. Ignoring the Waiting Period
Most health policies have a waiting period of 2 to 4 years for pre-existing illnesses. Plan ahead before you need coverage.

### 4. Buying Policies Solely for Tax Savings
Insurance should primarily serve as a financial safety net, not just a tax-deduction vehicle.

### 5. Not Reviewing Coverage Annually
Life changes—getting married, having kids, or buying a home means your insurance requirements change too.
